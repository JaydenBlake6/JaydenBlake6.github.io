Lemon Dream Cheesecake (No-Bake)

Prep Time: 20 Minutes

Cooking Time: 5 Minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Serves: 6

Indulge in this luscious no-bake lemon cheesecake, a perfect treat for lemon lovers. Made with a handful of simple ingredients, this dessert offers a creamy, zesty flavor that will tantalise your taste buds and impress your guests.

Ingredients:

  • 110g Digestive biscuits
  • 50g Butter
  • 25g Light brown soft sugar
  • 350g Mascarpone cheese
  • 75g Caster sugar
  • Zest of 1 Lemon
  • Juice of 2-3 Lemons (about 90ml)

Method:

Step 1:

Begin by crushing the digestive biscuits into fine crumbs using a food bag and a rolling pin, or simply pulse them in a food processor. In a saucepan, melt the butter, then remove from heat and mix in the brown sugar and biscuit crumbs until well combined.

Step 2:

Prepare a 20cm loose-bottomed cake tin by lining the base with baking parchment. Press the buttery biscuit mixture evenly into the bottom of the tin, then place it in the fridge to chill while you prepare the cheesecake filling.

Step 3:

In a mixing bowl, beat together the mascarpone cheese, caster sugar, lemon zest, and freshly squeezed lemon juice until the mixture is smooth and creamy. Spread this delightful filling over the chilled biscuit base and return to the fridge to set for at least two hours.
